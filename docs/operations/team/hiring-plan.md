# NIL Optic Hiring Plan

> TITAN VII: GARRISON -- Team Setup & Scaling
> Last updated: 2026-03-04

---

## Purpose

This document defines every hire across all four scaling phases for NIL Optic, including role definitions, team assignments, priority levels, key skills, compensation estimates, basketball expertise requirements, and the strategic rationale for each role in the context of NIL intelligence.

All hiring is anchored to the **basketball-first** go-to-market strategy. Phase 1 hires must be capable of shipping a basketball-focused product and closing Power 4 athletic department deals. Subsequent phases expand the team to support multi-sport coverage, enterprise sales, and full NCAA + professional sports adjacency.

| Value | Meaning | Hiring Signal |

1. Tell me about a time you used data to change a significant decision at your company.

## Compensation Philosophy

### Principles

1. **Competitive with market**: Target 60th-75th percentile for base salary in relevant markets (national remote for tech, sports industry for GTM)
2. **Equity-heavy for early hires**: Phase 1 hires receive meaningful equity grants; equity percentage decreases with phase but remains competitive
3. **Performance-aligned for sales**: OTE structures with achievable quotas; accelerators above 100% attainment
4. **Transparent bands**: Compensation bands shared internally starting Phase 2; external transparency by Phase 3
5. **Annual review cycle**: Annual compensation review tied to performance and market data; mid-year adjustment for retention-critical roles

### Equity Guidelines

| Phase | Equity Pool Access | Individual Grant Range (% of total pool) |
