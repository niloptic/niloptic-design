## Basketball-First Strategy

Basketball is the entry point for every institution. The sport drives the highest NIL valuations, commands the most media attention, and generates the strongest demand for NIL management tools. Every school in this registry is a basketball program first. Multi-sport expansion happens only after basketball onboarding is complete and active.

This document is the master registry of all NCAA basketball programs targeted by NIL Optic. It is the canonical source of truth for the Battle Board pipeline.

## Division III (Tier D)

### Summary

- **Conferences:** ~43 active D3 basketball conferences
- **Programs:** ~400+ men's basketball programs
- **Model:** Self-serve platform — no dedicated AE assignment
- **Expansion Timeline:** Post-D2 (target: late 2027 or 2028)

### D3 Considerations

- D3 athletes cannot receive athletic scholarships, but NIL rights still apply
- Growing NIL awareness at D3 level creates emerging demand
- Product must be adapted for lower-budget programs
- Community-tier pricing model required
- Key conferences: UAA, NESCAC, Centennial, Old Dominion Athletic

## NAIA (Tier D)

### Summary

- **Programs:** ~250 basketball programs across two divisions
- **Conferences:** ~25 conferences
- **Model:** Self-serve with community support
- **Expansion Timeline:** Long-term (2028+)

- Potential for bundled state-level deals (all NAIA programs in a state)
